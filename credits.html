Standing on the shoulders of giants

Credits

BannerHub is a patch bundle — almost nothing under the hood is ours. Every game launch rides on years of upstream open-source work. Huge thanks to everyone below. If an attribution is wrong, missing, or you'd like to be removed, tell us and we'll fix it.

Feature ports & pull requests we integrated

Specific contributors whose work BannerHub adapted (with permission where applicable).

🎮 Controller vibration / rumble

  • TideGear — the entire PC-accurate vibration patch: original rumble port (PR #80), the preload-free winebus.so rework (PR #91), and v6 menu icons + portrait dialog (PR #6). Via GameHub-Vibration-Fix.
  • GameNative — the upstream Wine-on-Android rumble lineage (PR #1214) TideGear's fix derives from.

🛒 GOG & Epic reliability

  • Utkarsh Dalal & GameNative contributors — GOG multi-CDN failover (PR #1220 / #1219) and Epic Online Services (PR #1286).
  • Jeremy Bernstein — GOG co-author (PR #1219).
  • Bart Zaalberg — GOG CDN picker (PR #1215).
  • Joshua Tam — resumable PARTIAL install state (PR #1277).

⚡ AI Frame Generation wiring

  • The GameHub team — the frame-interpolation engine itself.
  • teldommm — frame-gen onResume reliability + dialog cleanup (Bannerhub-Lite PR #5).

🧩 Community & tooling

  • The412Banner community — game configs, device testing & the BannerHub builds.
  • RobZombie9043 — the steam-files-ES-DE community library (front-end export fallback).
  • ImranR98 — Obtainium — the recommended in-app updater.
  • Crowdin translators — Japanese localization (3,534 strings).
